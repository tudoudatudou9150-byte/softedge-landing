Good to know before you buy

Short answers to common questions about the magnetic fridge organizer.

Is shipping included?

Yes. The listed price includes shipping and duties for supported destinations.

Will it stick to every fridge?

It works best on magnetic metal surfaces. It may not attach to glass, wood, or some non-magnetic stainless steel panels.

Is the logo printed on the organizer?

No. The logo is supplied as a sticker, so the final look may vary depending on how it is applied.
